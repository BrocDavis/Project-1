"Step Out!" is your guide to events and activities happening in your own backyard this very day. Well, maybe not literally in your backyard because that would be weird. But close by.

Just enter your ZIP code, how far you are willing to range, and "Step Out!" will show you some options. And also current weather conditions. So get off the couch, and step out!


Undecided? Not sure what to pick? Let "Step Out!" surprise you. (It's kind of like the bossy best friend who likes to plan your day for you, only with an Off switch.)
