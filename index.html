"Step Out!" is your guide to events and activities happening in your own backyard on this very day. Well, maybe not literally in your backyard because that would be weird. But close by.

Just enter your ZIP code, how far you are willing to range, and "Step Out!" will show you a lot of different options. And also current weather conditions, so you know what gear to take with you. So get off the couch, and step out!


Still undecided? Not sure which of these to pick? Let "Step Out!" choose for you. (It's kind of like the bossy best friend who likes to plan your day for you, only with an Off switch.)
